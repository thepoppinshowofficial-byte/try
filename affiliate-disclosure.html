Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how playzone-us earns income and how that affects the content you read on this site.

Home›Affiliate Disclosure
Effective Date: 1 June 2025

Summary: playzone-us participates in affiliate marketing programmes. Some links on this site may earn us a small commission if you click through and take an action — at no extra cost to you. This never influences what we write or recommend.

1. What is affiliate marketing?

Affiliate marketing is an arrangement where a website earns a commission by promoting products or services offered by a third party. When you click a link on playzone-us and subsequently make a purchase, sign up for a service, or complete another qualifying action on a partner's website, we may receive a small payment from that partner.

This is a common and transparent way for free content websites to earn revenue without charging readers or relying heavily on display advertising.

2. Which links may be affiliate links?

Affiliate links on playzone-us may appear in the following types of content:

  • Recommendations for fantasy basketball platforms and apps
  • Links to NBA streaming services and TV subscription packages
  • Guides referencing sports data or analytics tools
  • Any content where we reference a third-party service by name with a direct link

Where a link is part of an affiliate arrangement, we endeavour to make this clear with a disclosure notice near the relevant content. If you are ever uncertain whether a link is an affiliate link, please contact us and we will clarify.

3. Does this affect our editorial independence?

No. Our editorial content is written independently. We do not accept payment to write positive reviews, alter our opinions, or promote products we do not genuinely believe are relevant and fair for our readers.

Affiliate commissions are earned based on clicks and conversions — not based on what we say about a product. We would write the same guides whether or not affiliate arrangements existed.

🏀 Our commitment to you

playzone-us exists to give US basketball fans honest, free, high-quality NBA guides. We will never compromise the accuracy or integrity of our content in exchange for affiliate income. If we recommend something, it is because we genuinely think it is useful for fans — not because we are paid to say so.

4. FTC compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), playzone-us discloses any material connection between this website and any third party whose products or services are mentioned, linked to, or recommended on this site.

This disclosure page, along with inline disclosures placed near relevant affiliate content, constitutes our compliance with FTC requirements. If you have any concerns about our compliance, please contact us directly.

5. Sports streaming and fantasy platforms

playzone-us may include links to NBA League Pass, ESPN+, Amazon Prime Video, Yahoo Sports Fantasy, ESPN Fantasy, and other sports platforms. Some of these links may be affiliate links. The content of our guides about these platforms reflects our honest assessment of their features and value — not the existence of any commercial arrangement.

6. No additional cost to you

Clicking an affiliate link on playzone-us does not cost you anything extra. The price you pay for any product or service is the same whether you arrive via our link or any other source. Affiliate commissions are paid by the partner company from their own margin — not added to your purchase price.

7. Third-party sites

Once you click an affiliate link and leave playzone-us, you are subject to the terms, conditions and privacy policies of the third-party website you visit. We are not responsible for the content, practices or policies of any external site.

8. Changes to this disclosure

We may update this Affiliate Disclosure from time to time as our partnerships change. Updates take effect immediately upon posting. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions about our affiliate arrangements or this disclosure, please reach out via our Contact page. We are happy to clarify any specific link or partnership.
